About us

A youth-led non-profit organization that provides back to the youth with opportunities, skills, leadership, and experience that are crucial to life. We are "Aequitas" which is latin for eqaulity and that is all we are, since we ourselves are a youth team we are all growing together that is why we want to contribute to the growth of all youth and get them to places they want to be.
